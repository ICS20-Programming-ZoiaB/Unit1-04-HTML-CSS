The benefits of sleep on a developing mind

Why is sleep so crucial?

  • Sleep boosts the immune system, thus helping you get sick less often.
  • Getting adequate sleep reduces stress, which improves your mood and fuels healthy choices.
  • There is shown to be a correlation between number of hours slept and academic scores. Overall, the more sleep the students had, the more highly they scored on standardized mathematics tests.

Here is a table showing the positive correlation of sleep and academic achievement:

Hours of sleep: Average score (percentage) on math test:
